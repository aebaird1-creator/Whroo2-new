Webster, Ann


Rushworth Chronicle 3 September, 1886


A Magisterial Inquiry was held at Whroo yesterday by Mr Wigg, J.P., to ascertain the cause of death of Ann Webster, aged 60, who died suddenly on the previous evening.


The evidence of her husband and of a neighbour, Mrs Johnston, went to show that deceased had been ill for a long time.


A verdict of death from natural causes was returned.


The interment register at Whroo cemetery includes Ann Webster, buried 1886.
